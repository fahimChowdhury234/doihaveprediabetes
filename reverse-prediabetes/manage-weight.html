Start Wherever You Are​.

Reducing a modest amount of weight (10 - 15 pounds for a 200 - pound person) can effectively decrease the risk of developing type 2 diabetes.

Enhance awareness of your eating habits and their impact on your health by maintaining a food diary.

Involve your friends, coworkers, and family members in a collective effort for personal support.

Apple cider vinegar or supplements like α-MTPC may assist in managing or potentially reversing prediabetes.

Reversing prediabetes is achievable through a combination of increased physical activity, healthier dietary choices, and effective weight management.
